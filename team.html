Personal Trainer & Founder — Inspire Health and Fitness, LLC
Summer

Summer is the founder of Inspire Health and Fitness, LLC, and the creator of Inspire Fit Academy. Originally from West Monroe, Louisiana, she developed a passion for health and fitness that led her to earn certifications as a Certified Personal Trainer, Certified Fitness Instructor, Certified Nutrition Coach, and Certified Behavioral Specialist.

Summer is dedicated to helping women transform their bodies and minds so they can become confident role models for others. A proud mother of five, Summer lives in Cabot, Arkansas, with her husband, Michael, who serves at Little Rock Air Force Base. In her free time, she enjoys working out, running, swimming, hiking, and spending quality time outdoors with her family.
