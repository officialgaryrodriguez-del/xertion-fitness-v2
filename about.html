Our Story

A Gym Built
for Everyone

Xertion Fitness was built with one goal: give every person in Cabot — regardless of experience level — access to a world-class training environment at an honest price.

Under new management and better than ever, Xertion Fitness is a community. Whether you're picking up a dumbbell for the first time or training for your next competition, you'll find the equipment, the coaches, and the culture to help you reach your goals. The facility is open 24/7 with key fob access — train on your schedule, not ours.
